Our Brand Story

Maluca Villa is more than just a destination; it's a celebration of the natural beauty and cultural richness of Kalaw. Our brand spirit focuses on the people of Kalaw: humble, loving, and kind.

We believe in creating a pure connection with the warmth of human nature and the freshness of mother nature. Our products feature 100% natural ingredients ethically sourced from the Kalaw region.

At Maluca Villa, we're committed to building a positive and passionate team with family spirit, the same value we bring to our relationship with you, our guests.

Founder's Story & Go Green Initiative

Founded in 2018 in Kalaw, Maluca Villa started as a passion project to create Go Green experiences. Our journey began with a simple vision: to share the beauty of sustainable living with our community.

Why Kalaw?

We chose Kalaw because:

  • Shan State locals cultivate trees, plants, and crops abundantly
  • The fresh natural environment allows people to thrive in harmony with nature
  • Living surrounded by green forests that produce oxygen improves health and longevity
  • We believe in reducing environmental impact and improving our lifestyle

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We started practicing the three R's: Reduce – Reuse – Recycle to address global environmental challenges including:

  • Global warming and climate change
  • Ocean and land pollution
  • Waste disposal problems
  • Excessive energy consumption leading to carbon emissions
  • Loss of biodiversity

Join Our Green Movement

At Maluca Villa, we're committed to preserving Kalaw's natural beauty for future generations. Every small action counts toward a greener planet.

Discover Kalaw

Explore the charming hill station known for its cool climate and natural beauty

The Heart of Shan State

Kalaw is a picturesque hill town in the Shan State of Myanmar, situated at an elevation of 1,320 meters above sea level. Known for its cool climate throughout the year, it was once a popular British colonial retreat.

The town is surrounded by lush pine forests, terraced fields, and colorful flower gardens, creating a serene atmosphere that captivates visitors. Kalaw's unique blend of natural beauty, cultural diversity, and colonial charm makes it a must-visit destination.

What makes Kalaw truly special is its vibrant multicultural community, home to various ethnic groups including the Shan, Palaung, Pa-O, and Danu people.

Maluca Coffee

Nestled in the highlands of Shan State, Kalaw has become renowned for its exceptional coffee cultivation. At Maluca Villa, we celebrate this rich heritage by sourcing the finest local beans.

Our coffee journey begins with carefully selected beans from local plantations, where the cool climate and fertile soil create the perfect growing conditions. We work directly with farmers who share our commitment to sustainability.

Each cup of Maluca Villa coffee tells a story of tradition, quality, and the unique terroir of Kalaw. From bean to cup, we ensure that every step honors the natural environment.
