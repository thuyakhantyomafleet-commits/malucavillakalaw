Founder's Story & Go Green Initiative

Founded in 2018 in Kalaw, Maluca Villa started as a passion project to create Go Green experiences. Our journey began with a simple vision: to share the beauty of sustainable living with our community.

Why Kalaw?

We chose Kalaw because:

  • Shan State locals cultivate trees, plants, and crops abundantly
  • The fresh natural environment allows people to thrive in harmony with nature
  • Living surrounded by green forests that produce oxygen improves health and longevity
  • We believe in reducing environmental impact and improving our lifestyle

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We started practicing the three R's: Reduce – Reuse – Recycle to address global environmental challenges including:

  • Global warming and climate change
  • Ocean and land pollution
  • Waste disposal problems
  • Excessive energy consumption leading to carbon emissions
  • Loss of biodiversity

How We Go Green

At home: Reduce energy consumption, switch to solar power

In our restaurant: Replace plastic with sustainable alternatives like paper bags, brown paper, banana leaves, natural fabrics, and reusable containers

Individual actions: Use reusable cups, plates, and utensils instead of disposable plastics

Our Initiatives

We encourage everyone to:

  • Form groups for tree planting activities
  • Organize "No Plastic Week" campaigns
  • Install recycling bins throughout our facilities
  • Adopt new technologies like smart home systems and electric vehicles
  • Start small - even tiny changes can lead to big transformations
  • Share the message with others to multiply our impact

By starting today, we can all enjoy fresh air, good health, and a sustainable future. Remember: "If you can't do much, starting small can lead to big changes. Share the message with others."

Discover Kalaw

Explore the charming hill station known for its cool climate and natural beauty

The Heart of Shan State

Kalaw is a picturesque hill town in the Shan State of Myanmar, situated at an elevation of 1,320 meters above sea level. Known for its cool climate throughout the year, it was once a popular British colonial retreat and remains a favorite destination for those seeking respite from the heat.

The town is surrounded by lush pine forests, terraced fields, and colorful flower gardens, creating a serene atmosphere that captivates visitors. Kalaw's unique blend of natural beauty, cultural diversity, and colonial charm makes it a must-visit destination in Myanmar.

What makes Kalaw truly special is its vibrant multicultural community, home to various ethnic groups including the Shan, Palaung, Pa-O, and Danu people. This diversity is reflected in the town's rich cultural tapestry, traditional festivals, and warm hospitality.

Maluca Coffee

Nestled in the highlands of Shan State, Kalaw has become renowned for its exceptional coffee cultivation. At Maluca Villa, we celebrate this rich heritage by sourcing the finest local beans and crafting each cup with precision and care.

Our coffee journey begins with carefully selected beans from local plantations, where the cool climate and fertile soil create the perfect growing conditions. We work directly with farmers who share our commitment to sustainable and ethical practices.

Each cup of Maluca Villa coffee tells a story of tradition, quality, and the unique terroir of Kalaw. From bean to cup, we ensure that every step honors the natural environment and the skilled hands that cultivate these precious beans.
