About Maluca Villa

Our story rooted in the natural beauty and culture of Kalaw

Our Brand Story

Maluca Villa is more than just a destination; it's a celebration of the natural beauty and cultural richness of Kalaw. Our brand spirit focuses on the people of Kalaw: humble, loving, and kind.

We believe in creating a pure connection with the warmth of human nature and the freshness of mother nature. Our products feature 100% natural ingredients ethically sourced from the Kalaw region.

At Maluca Villa, we're committed to building a positive and passionate team with family spirit, the same value we bring to our relationship with you, our guests.

Culinary Excellence

At Maluca Villa's Restaurant & Bar, we pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Kalaw while embracing international flavors.

Our menu is a carefully curated selection of dishes that highlight the best of local ingredients. We work directly with farmers and producers in the Kalaw region to source the freshest, highest-quality ingredients for our kitchen.

Whether you're craving authentic Danu & Shan cuisine or looking for familiar European dishes, our talented chefs prepare each meal with passion and attention to detail.

Craft Cocktails

Our bar offers a selection of handcrafted cocktails made with premium spirits and fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Each cocktail tells a story of Kalaw's rich heritage and natural abundance.

Try our signature "Kalaw Sunset" - a refreshing blend of local rum, passion fruit, lime, and a hint of honey, garnished with edible flowers from our garden.
